# Allwinner

## Disclaimer

* **Only nightly releases are available right now. Use at your own risk!**
* **Boards/STBs with less than 1GiB of RAM are not supported**

## Boards Supported

### **A64 based boards**

* OrangePi Win
* Pine64
* Pine64 LTS
* Pine64 Plus

### **H3 based boards**

* BananaPi M2+
* Beelink X2
* Libretech ALL-H3-CC (Tritium)
* NanoPi M1
* OrangePi 2
* OrangePi PC
* OrangePi PC plus
* OrangePi Plus/Plus 2
* OrangePi Plus 2E

### H5 based boards

* OrangePi PC 2

### **H6 based boards**

* Beelink GS1
* OrangePi 3
* OrangePi 3 LTS
* OrangePi Lite 2
* OrangePi One Plus
* Pine H64
* Tanix TX6

### **R40 based boards**

* BananaPi M2 Ultra
